The Role

Essence Ad Operations oversees core media disciplines such as Campaign Management, Brand Safety, Product Development, Tech Billing and finally Creative Asset Management (CAM).

As a member of the Creative Asset Management team, you will be helping in the management of the London office’s creative assets. You will report into the Creative Asset Manager, learn several different parts of the Ad Operations Team’s remit, and will work to ensure the delivery of assets in a functioning and timely fashion.

Some of the things we’d like you to do:

  • Working closely with your manager to establish the new team as a core part of the Ad Operations offering. Working alongside the Operations team here in EMEA.
  • Help the team to maintain a high-quality standard for the Ad Operations department by overseeing Quality Assurance of creative.
  • Work outside of the CAM discipline to assist the wider Ad Operations team with trafficking; increasing trafficking knowledge from simple creative setups, encompassing standard desktop display, mobile, and 1x1 tracking, to more intricate creative setups, encompassing but not limited to video and dynamic creative
  • Help lead the team to continually identify and implement opportunities for improvement within team processes, supporting systems and communication channels.
  • Strive for excellence, build interdepartmental relationships, and work towards continuous personal professional improvement.
  • Support senior team members on complex campaign setups and escalations to third party vendors when necessary, and on large-scale projects
  • Act as an ambassador of ad operations across the agency. Work with senior leads in other teams to raise visibility, train new starters on the process, continually educate teams on recent developments; building advocacy across the agency.

A bit about yourself:

  • Preferably a graduate with a minimum BA from a reputable University or equivalent
  • Computer literate with superior knowledge of Excel and a strong interest in internet technologies and media
  • Able and eager to learn new concepts and challenge the existing framework
  • Diligent with strong attention to detail
  • Strong quantitative, analytical & problem-solving skills
  • An active interest in everything digital, ideally with an understanding of online advertising industry trends
  • A self-starter with the ability to manage your own workload
  • Excellent verbal & written communication skills
  • Experience of working with Doubleclick Dart, Mediaplex MOJO and/or other 3rd party ad servers would be a distinct advantage
  • Experience with Google docs a plus
  • Technical skills would be a distinct advantage; including HTML, JavaScript
